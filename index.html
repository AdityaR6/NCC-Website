WHY NCC ?



We Fire

For NCC cadets, one of the many perks of being part of the organization is the opportunity to experience firing rounds from a deluxe or sporting rifle of a caliber around 5.56mm. During the Annual Training Camp, cadets receive hands-on training from army personnel, providing them with a unique and thrilling experience.

We March

"कन्धों से मिलते हैं कंधे कदमों से कदम मिलते हैं, हम चलते हैं जब ऐसे तो दिल दुश्मन के हिलते हैं" Drills are about the whole unit. They are about competing to become the best while uplifting everyone else to do better. Drills teach us that unity is everything, and discipline is foremost. At NCC TCET, students are trained in drills by their seniors in sessions that take place once a week.

We Celebrate

NCC TCET encourages the celebration of festivals such as Dussehra, Diwali, Lohri, Makarsankranti, Holi, and many more. These celebrations are open to all students, creating a happy environment in the organization. These festivities allow students to unwind and relax while celebrating the richness of life.

We Tour

NCC provides exciting tours for cadets, such as challenging treks to the highest peak of Maharashtra, overnight camping followed by water sports, and a 40 km cycling trip to Marine Drive. These tours give cadets a sense of eliteness, as they get to visit military establishments not accessible to the general public. These tours allow cadets to satisfy their wanderlust and experience new adventures.

About Us

Adoption of the motto of NCC The motto of NCC is 'Unity & Discipline' which was adopted on 23 Dec 1957. In living up to its motto, the NCC strives to be and is one of the greatest cohesive forces of the nation, bringing together the youth hailing from different parts of the country and moulding them into united, secular and disciplined citizens of the nation.

3 Maharashtra Battalion NCC Mumbai Group “A” Established in 2021 . We Started with 34 cadets in total with help of Cdr. Vijay Pratap Singh sir . NCC TCET intents at developing patriotism, brotherhood and discipline in the cadets, thereby creating dynamic and visionary leaders of future India.

NCC TCET strives to fulfil the following aims:
  • Leadership Qualities
  • Personality Development
  • Motivation

The NCC is a responsive, learning and continuously evolving organization. Its activity is guided by certain Core Values that we endeavour to instill among all ranks of the NCC. These include the following:

  1. A sense of patriotic commitment to encourage cadets to contribute to national development.
  2. Respect for diversities in religion, language, culture, ethnicity, life style and habitat to instill a sense of National unity and social cohesion.
  3. Abiding commitment to learn and adhere to the norms and values enshrined in the Indian Constitution.
  4. Understanding the value of a just and impartial exercise of authority.
  5. Ability to participate in community development and other social programme.
  6. A healthy life style free of substance abuse and other unhealthy practices.
  7. Sensitivity to the needs of poor and socially disadvantaged fellow citizens.
  8. Inculcating habits of restraint and self-awareness.
  9. Understanding the values of honesty, truthfulness, self-sacrifice, perseverance and hard work.
  10. Respect for knowledge, wisdom and the power of ideas.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why NCC?

    "A thoughtful mind when it sees the national flag, sees not the flag, but the nation itself." - Henry Ward Beecher The trademark of NCC TCET is giving the rarest opportunities to its cadets. NCC bestows the providential opportunity of saluting the flag twice a year, on Independence Day and Republic Day. The cadets also get a chance to give the coveted Guard of Honour to the higher rank officers of defense forces. When cadets pass by the flag, with uniform and ceremonials put on, the heartbeats synchronize with the beats of the march past, the salute becoming a reflex and eyes of the audience on them, there is no better feeling than that!

  • What are the perks to be in NCC?

    Physical fitness, sense of discipline and untiy along with that exemption in couple of exam.

  • What is the procedure to be in NCC?

    Qualify the physical test and fill the required form .

  • Is there any fees to join?

    No, it's absolutely free to join but there will be nominal fees for joining which will be furbished later on completely.

  • What are the Activity conducted?

    On different occasions there will be an event/Fallin and there will be few other activities i.e.- beach cleaning, donation drive and few other social works
